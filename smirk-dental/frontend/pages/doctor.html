    About Dr. Mehak Gupta

    With over 17 years of dedicated clinical practice, Dr. Mehak Gupta is one of South Delhi's most trusted and respected dental surgeons. Her journey began with a deep passion for restoring smiles — and that passion continues to drive every treatment she delivers.

    Dr. Gupta holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) degree and has built her expertise across a wide spectrum of dentistry — from routine preventive care to complex aesthetic and implant procedures. She stays at the forefront of dental innovation through continuous learning and advanced training.

    Her philosophy is simple: "Every patient deserves to smile confidently. My job is to make that happen safely, comfortably, and beautifully." This patient-first mindset is why she has earned a loyal patient base of families across Vasant Kunj and beyond.
